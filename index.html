About

Your Resume Reimagined

In a competitive job market, a strong resume is more than a list of qualifications—it must clearly convey impact. HR professionals, often unfamiliar with the intricacies of the roles they hire for, rely on predefined criteria. Without a well-structured resume, even highly qualified candidates risk being overlooked.


To address this challenge, Akiva Futter leverages 5+ years of experience in Product and Tech to apply a product-driven approach to resume optimization. By strategically highlighting achievements, structuring responsibilities effectively, and ensuring a clean, professional design, he crafts resumes that capture attention and unlock career opportunities.

Our Process

Step 1: Intro Call

We start with a personalized consultation to understand your career goals, answer any questions, and outline how our process aligns with your needs.

Step 2: Consulting Session

We take a deep dive into your experience, highlighting not just what you did but the impact you made. We uncover key achievements and strengths to position you as a top candidate.

Step 3: Resume Rebuild

Using insights from our consulting session, we craft a modern, compelling resume tailored to how hiring teams assess candidates. Your new resume will be sleek, strategic, and optimized for maximum impact.

Step 4: Follow-Up Session

We review your resume together to ensure it effectively tells your professional story, refining details to highlight your unique strengths and value.

Step 5: Application Coaching

We equip you with expert strategies on how to leverage your new resume, optimize job applications, and increase your chances of securing interviews.

Step 6: Ongoing Check-ins

Need adjustments? We’ve got you covered! For up to three months, we’ll provide ongoing support, refining your resume or application approach to help you achieve the best results.
